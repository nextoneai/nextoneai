How it works

Step 1

Connect & Collect

Connect your profile or start with dummy JSON data. We ingest jobs and people data, then normalize skills and tags.

Step 2

Build Knowledge Graphs

We construct two graphs: KG1 (Jobs/Tasks/Skills) and KG2 (People/Skills/Preferences) using Neo4j.

Step 3

Sequence & Match

Our engine scores skills fit and sequences jobs into packets that you can do solo or with a team for higher earnings.
